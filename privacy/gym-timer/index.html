Privacy Policy

The Application does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information directly from users.

All timer settings, workout routines, and related data are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to or stored on any external servers operated by the Service Provider.

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

The Service Provider does not sell, trade, or rent user data. Any data collected through advertising services is handled by Google in accordance with their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

Since the Application does not collect personal data directly, the Service Provider does not retain user data. Any locally stored data remains on your device until you delete the Application.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain data protection rights, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. For data collected through advertising services, please refer to Google’s privacy policy.
